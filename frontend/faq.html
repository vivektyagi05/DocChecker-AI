Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about DocChecker.

What document types do you support?

We support PDFs, PNGs, JPGs, and JPEGs. This covers offer letters, lease agreement scans, NDAs, and business papers.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All uploads are stored in restricted user directories. Under our API license with Google, your files are never used to train models.

How accurate is the risk score?

Our audits are powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash and compute scores based on strict risk criteria. However, DocChecker is an assistant and does not constitute formal legal advice.
